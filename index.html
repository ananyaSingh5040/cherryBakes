About Cherry – My Baking Journey

Well, well… Hello there! I'm Cherry, and I truly appreciate that you want to know more about me. It warms my heart! So, how did my baking journey begin? It all started with one simple yet magical chocolate cake recipe—made entirely with Oreos! I was beyond excited to try it, and little did I know, that single experiment would spark a deep love for baking. Now, let me be clear—I’m not a certified baker, nor do I have a formal degree in pastry arts. I’m simply a passionate home baker who finds pure joy in mixing, whisking, and creating sweet delights. Baking, for me, is more than just a skill; it’s a special hobby that brings me happiness and comfort. From my first Oreo cake to trying out different flavors and techniques, every baking experience has been a learning journey. Sometimes, things go perfectly, and sometimes… well, let’s just say the oven and I have had our fair share of disagreements. But that’s the beauty of baking—it’s all about experimenting, learning, and enjoying the process! Through this website, I want to share my love for baking with you. Whether you’re here for a simple, beginner-friendly recipe or just want to explore the world of sweet treats, I hope my journey inspires you to grab an apron and start baking! So, welcome to my little baking world! Let’s create something delicious together.
